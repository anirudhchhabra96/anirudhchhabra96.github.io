About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Cincinnati, conducting research in the Intelligent Autonomous Systems Research Laboratory (IASRL). My primary work focuses on building hardware-in-the-loop spacecraft simulators to test on-orbit service missions.

Beyond this, my research spans autonomous systems, multi-agent navigation, fault-tolerant spacecraft control, and sensor fusion. I enjoy designing, building, and experimenting with systems that bridge theory and practice in robotics and aerospace.
