Teaching Experience



Instructor — Intelligent Robotics (AEEM 6117)

Institution: Department of Aerospace Engineering, University of Cincinnati, Cincinnati, OH, USA

Dates: Summer 2024, Spring 2025

Course Details: Taught the graduate-level course on Intelligent Robotics to MEng (Online) students, covering fundamental theory and algorithms of robot dynamics and control, intelligent control of robotic systems using Reinforcement Learning, and Genetic Fuzzy Systems. Students completed a final project on multi-robot intelligent control.

Feedback: Received highly positive feedback (4.58 on a 5-Point Likert Scale). A few students joined research groups, inspired by the course, and one graduate student now contributes to robotic arm control in space under my guidance.

Update: Due to positive feedback, I was invited to teach the course again in Spring 2025, with 37 students from various programs (online and offline).
